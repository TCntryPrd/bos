## 1. PURPOSE

The Receiving Party (QIQ) is being engaged by the Disclosing Party (DCS) as a development partner to assist with the design, development, and delivery of the BodyshopConnect AI Platform ("BCAI") for Micazen Consulting & Technologies Inc. ("Micazen" / "End Client").

DCS operates under a separate Non-Disclosure Agreement with Micazen dated March 18, 2026 (the "Upstream NDA"). This Agreement extends the protections of the Upstream NDA to cover QIQ's access to Micazen's Confidential Information.

The Receiving Party acknowledges that:
- Access is granted solely for the purpose of performing development work on the BCAI project under DCS's direction.
- All obligations in this Agreement flow from and are at least as restrictive as the Upstream NDA between DCS and Micazen.
- Breach of this Agreement by QIQ constitutes breach of the Upstream NDA by DCS, exposing DCS to liquidated damages of CAD $250,000 per breach (escalating to CAD $1,000,000 for subsequent breaches).

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" includes, without limitation:

- All source code, object code, architecture, APIs, databases, and schemas of the BodyshopConnect system
- Algorithms, logic flows, system designs, and development methodologies
- Product features, UI/UX designs, workflows, and business logic
- Integrations, automation processes, and third-party connector implementations
- Customer data structures, tenant architecture, and data isolation mechanisms
- Business information including customer lists, pricing, revenue data, network agreements, and go-to-market strategy
- All documentation, notes, specifications, assessments, and derivatives created from the above
- All materials shared by DCS relating to the Micazen engagement including but not limited to: phase specifications, governance documents, strategic briefs, SOWs, meeting transcripts, and email correspondence

Confidential Information also includes anything learned, observed, inferred, or derived from reviewing or working with the system.

## 3. STRICT LIMITATION OF USE

The Receiving Party agrees:

- To use the Confidential Information **solely** for performing development work on the BCAI project under DCS's direction
- Not to use the information for:
  - Building, modifying, or improving any competing product
  - Internal development beyond the BCAI project scope
  - Commercial gain of any kind outside the BCAI engagement
  - Any purpose not expressly authorized by DCS in writing

## 4. NO REUSE / NO DERIVATIVES

The Receiving Party shall not:

- Reuse any portion of code, architecture, or design created for the BCAI project for any other client or purpose
- Reuse underlying concepts, structures, or implementations derived from the Confidential Information
- Create derivative works for any third party based on the BCAI project or related Confidential Information
- Retain any code, documentation, or materials after the engagement ends

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OWNERSHIP

All work product created by the Receiving Party in connection with the BCAI project shall be:

- **Owned exclusively by Micazen Consulting & Technologies Inc.** (per the Upstream NDA and SOW)
- Deemed "work made for hire" to the fullest extent permitted by law
- Assigned irrevocably to Micazen via DCS

The Receiving Party:
- Waives any moral rights (to the extent permitted by law)
- Agrees not to assert any ownership, lien, or claim over the work product
- Acknowledges that ownership vests immediately upon creation, whether or not the work is completed

**Exception:** General-purpose development tools, frameworks, and methodologies that existed prior to this engagement and are not derived from Confidential Information remain the property of their respective owners. Any such pre-existing tools must be identified in writing before use in the BCAI project.

## 6. ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION — REVERSE ENGINEERING & COMPETITION

The Receiving Party shall NOT, directly or indirectly:

- Reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or attempt to extract source code from any Micazen system
- Recreate, replicate, or imitate any part of the BodyshopConnect system
- Develop derivative works based on the Confidential Information
- Use any learned concepts, structures, or methodologies to:
  - Build a competing product in the collision repair management space
  - Assist any third party in doing so
- Share insights with any competitor of Micazen or DCS

This restriction applies whether or not the information is memorized or recorded.

**No Residual Knowledge Exception:** The Receiving Party expressly agrees that it shall not use, rely upon, or exploit any Confidential Information retained in memory. The use of such retained knowledge for product development, system design, competitive offerings, or consulting services shall constitute a breach.

## 7. ACCESS CONTROL

The Receiving Party agrees:

- Only individuals who are directly performing work on the BCAI project may access Confidential Information
- Each such individual must acknowledge and agree to be bound by terms no less restrictive than this Agreement prior to gaining access
- The Receiving Party shall maintain a written log of all individuals who have accessed Confidential Information and provide this log to DCS upon request
- Access to Micazen's GitLab repository, codebase, or systems shall be through credentials provided and managed by DCS only

## 8. DATA HANDLING AND SECURITY

The Receiving Party agrees to:

- Store all Confidential Information on encrypted devices (AES-256 minimum)
- Not transfer Confidential Information to personal devices, cloud storage, or unsecured systems
- Not share Confidential Information via unencrypted email or messaging
- Use multi-factor authentication on all accounts that access project materials
- Not use any AI tool (including but not limited to ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, or code completion tools) to process, analyze, or generate outputs based on Micazen source code unless expressly authorized by DCS in writing
- Immediately notify DCS of any security incident or suspected breach

## 9. NON-CIRCUMVENTION & NON-SOLICITATION

The Receiving Party agrees:

- Not to contact Micazen, Sharon Ashley, Jim Wraight, or any Micazen employee, contractor, client, or partner directly for any purpose related to this project or otherwise, without DCS's express written consent
- Not to solicit or accept work from Micazen independently, whether during or after this engagement, for a period of 24 months
- Not to solicit employees or contractors of DCS for 24 months
- All communication with the End Client flows through DCS exclusively

## 10. RETURN / DESTRUCTION

- All Confidential Information must be returned or securely destroyed within 7 days
